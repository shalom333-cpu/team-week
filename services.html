HOTEL LISTINGS

INDIGO HOTEL

WESTLANDS - NAIROBI

Indigo Hotel located in Westlands Nairobi features air-conditioned rooms with satellite flat-screen TV in the Westlands, Nairobi. The property is within 400 m of Exhibition Hall. Free WiFi is available.

At the hotel, every room is equipped with a desk. All rooms are equipped with a private bathroom with free toiletries and a hairdryer. Guest rooms include a wardrobe.

Guests can choose from the continental, vegetarian and Asian breakfast options served daily at the property.



GRAND PLAZA

LAVINGTON - NAIROBI

Located in Lavington, Grand Plaza Hotel offers free WiFi access in all areas. Other facilities offered at the property include a ticket service, a tour desk and luggage storage.

Featuring city views each room comes with a TV and air conditioning. Rooms are fitted with a shower and private bathroom.

Guests can enjoy local and international cuisine at the onsite restaurant. A range of drinks is served at the bar.



UPPER HILLS GRAND HOTEL

UPPER HILL - NAIROBI

Offering a terrace and views of the city, Upper Hills Grand Hotel is located in the Upper Hills district in Nairobi. Guests can enjoy the on-site restaurant. Free WiFi is offered throughout the property and private parking is available on site.

Every room comes with a flat-screen TV. The units boast a sitting area and feature a desk and a safe. All rooms come with a private bathroom. Extras include free toiletries and a hairdryer.

Guests can choose from the continental, full English/Irish, vegan and vegetarian breakfast options available daily at the property.



SHERATON HOTEL
