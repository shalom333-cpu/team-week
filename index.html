Welcome to Laguna Resort

A luxury beachfront resort with traditional Swahili charm, Laguna Resort is situated on the finest white sandy beach. Set in 30-acres of tropical gardens with over 1,000 palm trees, the resort is one of the most prestigious properties offering the ultimate comfort: excellent accommodation; fine wining and dining; a host of sports and leisure facilities; the famous Oasis Spa; and our award-winning service. Free WiFi is also available throughout the Resort.

As well as being a magical destination for holidaymakers wishing to soak up the sun. The resort also offers excellent facilities for meetings, conferences and group incentives. Our weddings and events team can also plan your private party, your wedding ceremony and reception and, of course, your honeymoon.
